NetWorked!: A Gamified E-Learning Mobile
Application for Internetworking Course

Project Overview

NetWorked! is an innovative e-learning application designed to educate computer science students about the foundation or basic of network syllabus, with a specific focus on the five layers of the TCP/IP model. NetWorked! seeks to improve learning outcomes by improving student engagement, motivation and retention through the use of interactive game design elements and gamification approaches.

The application contains four separate levels, each of which focuses on one of the five TCP/IP layers, including topics covering the Physical and Data Link layers, Network layer, Transport layer, and Application layer. NetWorked! offers a more immersive and engaging learning experience with its dynamic and effective approach to network education.

Key Features

Structured Layer-Based Levels

Master the TCP/IP model step-by-step through four focused levels, which each dedicated to a specific layer group for clear and progressive learning.

Immersive Gamified Learning

Stay engaged with interactive game features like points, avatars, timed challenges and achievements that make studying feel like play.

Practical Academic Study Tool

Strengthen student's internetworking knowledge with a portable question bank that doubles as a reliable study companion for academic success.

Challenges & Solutions

Identified Challenges

Existing e-learning tools for the subject were passive and unengaging, leading to low student motivation.

The core material (TCP/IP layers) is often seen as abstract and difficult to contextualize.

The goal was to move beyond a simple quiz format to create a truly immersive learning experience.

Solutions & Action Plan

Designed and developed 4 distinct game levels, and each mapped to a group of TCP/IP layers to provide thematic context.

Implemented key gamification mechanics including points system, player avatar or character and timer to boost engagement.

Developed a narrative structure that aligns learning objectives with storytelling, which transforming study into an engaging and playful experience.
